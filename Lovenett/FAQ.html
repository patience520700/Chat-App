Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about LðŸ’žvenett

1. How do I create an account?
Click on "Sign Up" and enter your email, username, and password. Verify your email to activate your account.
2. I forgot my password. What should I do?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email.
3. How do I start a new chat?
Click the "+" or "New Chat" button and select the contact you want to message.
4. Can I send images and files?
Yes! Click the attachment icon (paperclip) in the chat to send images, documents, and other files.
5. How do I create a group chat?
Go to "New Group", select participants, name your group, and click "Create".
6. Is my data secure?
Yes, we use end-to-end encryption for all messages to protect your privacy.
7. How do I block a user?
Open the chat with the user, click their profile, and select "Block User".
8. Can I use the app on multiple devices?
Yes, your account syncs across all your devices when you log in.
9. How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Note: This action is permanent.
10. Why can't I see my messages?
Check your internet connection. If the problem persists, try logging out and back in.
11. How do I change my profile picture?
Go to your profile in Settings and click on your current picture to upload a new one.
12. Can I recover deleted messages?
No, once messages are deleted they cannot be recovered.
13. How do I report inappropriate content?
Long-press the message and select "Report" or contact our support team.
14. What's the maximum file size I can send?
The current limit is 100MB per file.
15. How do I turn off notifications?
Go to Settings > Notifications and adjust your preferences.
16. Can I use the app without a phone number?
Yes, you can register with just an email address.
17. How do I search for old messages?
Use the search bar at the top of the chat list to find specific messages.
18. Why is my contact not appearing?
Ensure they've registered for the app and you have the correct phone number/username.
19. How do I change my username?
Go to Settings > Profile > Username to make changes.
20. Where can I suggest new features?
We welcome suggestions! Email us at feedback@lovenett.com or use the in-app feedback form.
