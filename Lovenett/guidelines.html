Community Guidelines

Respect Everyone

  • ✅ Be kind and respectful in all interactions.
  • ❌ No harassment, bullying, or hate speech.
  • ❌ No discrimination based on race, gender, religion, etc.
  • ✅ Respect different opinions and perspectives.
  • ❌ No threats or violent language.

Content Rules

  • ✅ Post appropriate and relevant content.
  • ❌ No nudity, sexual content, or explicit images.
  • ❌ No violent, illegal, or harmful posts.
  • ❌ No spam, scams, or misleading information.
  • ✅ Keep conversations constructive and friendly.

Privacy & Safety

  • ✅ Protect your personal information.
  • ❌ No sharing of private data (phone numbers, addresses, etc.).
  • ❌ No impersonation or fake accounts.
  • ✅ Report suspicious behavior to moderators.
  • ❌ No unauthorized commercial activities.

Consequences of Violations

  • ⚠️ First offense: Warning and content removal.
  • ⚠️ Repeat offenses: Temporary or permanent ban.
  • ⚠️ Severe violations: Immediate account suspension.
  • ⚠️ Appeals: Contact support@lovenett.com for review.

Additional Notes

  • These guidelines apply to all users equally.
  • Moderators have final say in content decisions.
  • Guidelines may be updated periodically.
